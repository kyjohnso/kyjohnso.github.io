Study Materials

Interactive Learning Resources

🎲 Surprise me!
📈 Graph Lab
Slide the gradient and intercept — or drag the line itself — and watch y = mx + c come alive. An interactive playground for straight-line graphs.
⚛️ Atom Lab
Add protons, neutrons and electrons and the atom tells you what element, isotope and ion you've built. Explore atomic structure hands-on.
⚖️ Balance Lab
Keep the scales balanced: whatever you do to one side, do to the other — until x is on its own. A visual way to learn solving equations.
⭕ Circle Lab
Draw circles and arcs, cut pieces away, and see the area & perimeter equations build themselves. Master circle geometry interactively.
🔌 Circuit Lab
Drag parts onto the board, turn the dials, and watch the current flow. Build and test simple circuits with live voltage, resistance and current readings.
📐 Angle Lab
Drag the angles and the rules stay true, so the numbers always add up. Explore angles on lines, around points, and inside triangles.
🔺 Pythagoras Lab
a² + b² = c² — see why, with a real square built on every side of a right-angled triangle.
🔢 Factor Lab
Break numbers into their prime factors, find the HCF and LCM, and explore squares, cubes and roots.
➗ Fraction Lab
See fractions as bars, then add, subtract, multiply, divide and simplify them — visually.
💯 Number Lab
Fractions, decimals, percentages, rounding and proportion — see how they all connect.
🧱 Element Lab
The periodic table, where every element is one kind of atom. Tap any element to meet it.
⚗️ Compound Lab
Read chemical formulae, count the atoms, and learn to spot elements, compounds and mixtures.
🧪 Acid Lab
Explore the pH scale and indicators, and watch acids and alkalis cancel each other out.
🔬 Cell Lab
The building blocks of life — tap each part of the cell to find out what its job is.
🔋 Bioenergetics Lab
Photosynthesis and respiration: how living things make and use their energy.
🌍 Ecosystem Lab
Food chains, energy flow, and how every living thing depends on the others.
🐺 Predator-Prey Ecosystem
A cellular automaton simulator with caribou, wolves, and plants. Watch population cycles emerge, adjust parameters with sliders, and explore the experiments from your B2 predator-prey worksheet!
🌱 Photosynthesis & Respiration
Interactive molecule guide with drag-and-drop challenges! Build the reactions for photosynthesis, aerobic respiration, and anaerobic respiration using ball-and-stick molecule models.
🇫🇷 Lower 4 French Test
A Year 8 practice test: 25 random questions from a bank of 200, covering shops, quantities, verbs, the time, the near future, transport, countries, holidays, opinions, school and daily routine. Both directions, with a hint on every wrong answer.
French Grammar
50 cards covering verb conjugations (aller, partir, sortir, dormir) and prepositions with countries. Practice and master French grammar!
Hinduism Vocabulary
50 essential terms with interactive flashcards. Tap to flip and learn — plus a 25-question quiz!
Sikhism Vocabulary
50 essential terms covering the Gurus, the Khalsa, the 5 Ks, worship and beliefs. Tap to flip and learn — plus a 25-question quiz!
Islam Vocabulary
32 essential terms covering the Five Pillars, beliefs, festivals and key practices. Tap to flip and learn — plus a 25-question quiz!
Resurrection Vocabulary
50 essential terms on the death & resurrection of Jesus, life after death, karma, and humanism. Tap to flip and learn — plus a 25-question quiz!
🎲 All Religions Quiz
A combined 25-question quiz drawing randomly from all four sets — Hinduism, Sikhism, Islam and Christianity. Each question is colour-coded by religion.
